I have been the faculty at IIT Bombay since oct 1999. Before that I was a visiting professor at the University of New Mexico, Albuquerque and University of Maryland, College Park. My early work was in the area if Parallel Processing and Reliability. Since the last 15 years, my focus has been on two areas: Network Security & Cryptography and Smart E-Business and Forecasting.

I earned a B.Tech. from IIT-Bombay in Electrical Engineering, M.S. from University of Notre Dame and Ph.D. from University of Texas, Austin in Electrical and Computer Engineering.

I am teaching CS416, CS416(M), CS742 - Foundations of Network Security & Cryptography (Autumn 2015).
